Welcome to the homepage for the San Antonio Dental Hygienists' Association (SADHA). Our organization serves the greater San Antonio area providing guidance related to our profession.

SADHA Mission Statement

To improve the public's total health, the mission of the American Dental Hygienists' Association is to advance the art and science of dental hygiene by ensuring access to quality oral health care, increasing awareness of the cost-effective benefits of prevention, promoting the highest standards of dental hygiene education, licensure, practice and research, and representing and promoting the interests of dental hygienists.

Membership benefits

Membership provides access to all meetings and opportunities to earn Continuing Education (CE) credits which SADHA hosts throughout the year. ADHA members may attend CEs at no charge, while guest attendance is $25 per event.

Please see the Calendar for information about upcoming meetings.
