Membership

Your ADHA dues include automatic membership in TDHA and SADHA components. These entities organize events and meetings to enhance your personal and professional growth. Engage in local, state, and national gatherings to foster valuable connections and expand your professional network.

Becoming a member opens the door for you to participate in all our meetings and gain Continuing Education (CE) credits hosted by SADHA throughout the year. As a valued ADHA member, our quarterly CEs come at no additional cost to you. For those who aren't members but wish to join us, guest attendance is available for a modest fee of $35 per event. We'd be thrilled to have you onboard for an enriching experience of learning and networking.

Benefits of membership include

  • Access to continuing education courses
  • Journal of Dental Hygiene (digital subscription)
  • Travel discounts
  • Insurance coverage
  • Networking opportunities
  • See a full list

Members may apply for scholarships and research grants through the ADHA Foundation. The ADHA Foundation encourages and promotes professional excellence through scholarships and quality research in collaboration with ADHA.
