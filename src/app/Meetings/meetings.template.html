General Meetings

The SADDHA General Meetings are held via virtual Go To Meeting platform. Held every month. The General Meeting starts at 7:00 PM. Please note, there is NO C.E. Course at the General Meeting. C.E. Course are held on a different date of every month. Come join in the fun, the networking, the camaraderie and the service to our community!

Link to the General Meeting will be sent out to all SADHA Members and Non-Members by your SADHA Secretary, Jessica Duncan.
