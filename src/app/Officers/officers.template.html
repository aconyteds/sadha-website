2020-2021 SADHA Officers

 


Andrea S. Wiseman, RDH - President

Is the current President for San Antonio Dental Hygienists' Association. Andrea has been a member of the American Dental Hygienists' Association since 1995, as well as, Texas Dental Hygienists' Association and past president of the San Antonio Dental Hygienists' Association (2004) and Gulf Coast Dental Hygiene Society of Ft. Myers (1996-97), FL. She is a mentor to all oral healthcare providers and an advocate against oral diseases.

Andrea Wiseman is a Preventive Consultant Specialist for Elevate Oral Care and brings over 30 years of experience in dentistry to her presentations which includes webinars and lunch-n-learns on the Current Prevention Measures.

Please feel free to reach out to me if you have any questions at sadhapresident@gmail.com.

 

 

Mercedes Mendoza  -  President-Elect


Mercedes graduated from Concorde Career College with her Associate's degree in Dental Hygiene in June 2013. She also holds a Bachelor of Arts Degree in English and Communication Arts from St. Mary's University. She is a proud Rattler and an "only" child. Mercedes enjoys attending RDHUOR with her RDH Mama. She currently work full time for CommuniCare Health Centers and an occasional Saturday at a general private practice office.

At Mercedes' full-time job, the general patient population are those who have not seen the dentist, ever, or “it’s been awhile.” The most important part of her position is reviewing OHI with her patients and the greatest compliment that she receives is, “Wow, my teeth have never felt this clean.” She is very grateful and thanks her mom for her love of dental hygiene, "I would’ve never tried had she not pushed me to follow in her career path". When not working, Mercedes enjoys running marathons and making crafts for herself or her friends. She is a season ticket member for the San Antonio Spurs and loves to cheer them on.

Jodie Hostetter -  Trustee

Jodie Hostetter, RDH, BSDH is a 2010 graduate of the University of Texas Health Science Center at San Antonio.  While at UTHSCSA, she served as an active member of the Student American Dental Hygienists’ Association (SADHA), on various committees, and as the SADHA President.  Jodie has been appointed adjunct faculty at UTHSCSA since August 2012, where she supervises dental hygiene students working in a public health setting.  

She has been an active member in the San Antonio component since graduation and serves on various committees that include Public Relations, Oral Cancer Walk, Budget and Finance, and a member of the Board.  She also has the privilege to be a member of the San Antonio District Dental Society’s Smiles of Hope committee, the Texas Oral Health Coalition, and the American Association of Public Health Dentistry.  In the Texas Dental Hygienists’ Association board she serves as Area Media Spokesperson committee member and Public Affairs committee member.

Jodie has always been passionate about the community and public health.  She is currently a dental hygienist in the public sector for the San Antonio Metropolitan Health District and the Department of State Health Services.  Jodie is a single mom of a beautiful and wonderful daughter.  She spends her free time cycling on the open road, at the tennis courts with her daughter, or in her hometown of Fredericksburg with her family.  If you are looking to find out more about SADHA or a cycling partner, please contact her at sadhatrustee@gmail.com.
